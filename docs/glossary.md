# Glossary

This glossary contains Hindi and industry-specific terms used throughout the Cold Storage Management System documentation.

---

## A

### Aarti
Commission agent who facilitates transactions between farmers and traders.

### Aadhar
Aadhaar - India's 12-digit unique identity number issued by UIDAI.

### Amad
Incoming stock; the process of receiving goods into cold storage from farmers.

---

## B

### Bardana
Jute bags or packaging materials provided to farmers for storing produce. Often given as advance with interest.

### Bhugtaan
Payment made to a party; a type of voucher for disbursing funds.

### Bilti
Lorry receipt or transport document; proof of goods handed to transporter.

---

## C

### Chitti
Delivery receipt or slip issued when goods leave the cold storage facility.

### Contra
A voucher type for internal transfers between cash and bank accounts.

---

## D

### DR (Debit)
Debit entry in accounting; indicates amount owed by a party or amount paid out.

### DR Limit
Maximum debit (credit extended) allowed for a party.

### Dump
Category of incoming stock that is not fresh/straight; may be damaged or lower quality.

### Due Days
Number of days after which payment becomes overdue.

---

## G

### GST
Goods and Services Tax - India's indirect tax on supply of goods and services.

### Gurranter / Guarantor
A person who guarantees payment on behalf of another party.

---

## I

### IFSC
Indian Financial System Code - 11-character code identifying bank branches.

---

## K

### Katai
Cut or sorted produce; processed goods (as opposed to fresh/straight).

### Kissan
Farmer; primary depositor of goods in cold storage.

---

## L

### Lot
A batch or consignment of goods belonging to a specific party, stored together.

---

## M

### Mandi
Agricultural market where produce is traded.

### Marfat
Through or via; indicates an intermediary or agent in a transaction.

### Marka
Mark or brand; identification label on goods/bags.

---

## N

### Nikasi
Outgoing stock; the process of dispatching goods from cold storage.

### Nikasi Bill
Formal bill generated for dispatched goods including rent and charges.

---

## P

### PAN
Permanent Account Number - 10-character alphanumeric tax identification in India.

### Parcha
Document or slip; commonly refers to party statement.

### Pkts
Packets or bags; unit of counting stored goods.

---

## Q

### QTL
Quintal; unit of weight equal to 100 kilograms.

---

## R

### Reloading
Internal transfer of stock from one storage room to another.

---

## S

### Sauda
Deal or transaction agreement between a farmer (seller) and trader (buyer).

### Sauda Limit
Maximum transaction value allowed for a party.

### Seedhi
Straight or fresh; unprocessed incoming goods in original condition.

### Subgroup
Category classification for parties (e.g., FARMER, SUNDRY DEBTORS, STAFF).

---

## T

### TIN
Tax Identification Number (legacy; largely replaced by GST).

---

## V

### Voucher
Financial transaction record; includes payment, receipt, journal, and contra entries.

### Vyapari
Trader or merchant; buyer of goods stored in cold storage.

---

## Units & Measurements

| Unit | Full Form | Value |
|------|-----------|-------|
| QTL | Quintal | 100 kg |
| Pkts | Packets/Bags | Variable (typically 50-60 kg each) |
| pm | Per Month | Monthly rate |

|------|--------|---------|
| Contra | F4 | Cash to bank transfers |
| Payment | F5 | Outgoing payments |
| Receipt | F6 | Incoming receipts |
| Journal | F7 | Non-cash adjustments |
| Bhugtaan | F8 | Payments to parties |

---

## Stock Categories

| Category | Type | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Seedhi | Amad | Fresh, unprocessed incoming stock |
| Dump | Amad | Lower quality or damaged incoming stock |
| Seedhi | Nikasi | Fresh stock being dispatched |
| Katai | Nikasi | Processed/sorted stock being dispatched |
